I am a Doctoral student in Agricultural Economics and interested in how data science can help to better understand farmers’ crop choices, land use and irrigation practices. Being curious about the application of (Bayesian) econometrics, machine learning and satellite imagery, I want to contribute to better agricultural and environmental policy making.
